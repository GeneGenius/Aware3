Currency
The local currency is the Swazi Lilangeni (SZL), pegged 1:1 to the South African Rand (ZAR), which is widely accepted. ATMs available in Mbabane.

Weather in October
October is the start of summer in Eswatini — warm and sunny (24–28°C / 75–82°F) with occasional afternoon showers. Light layers for evenings.
